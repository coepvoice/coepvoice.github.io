My Ekadashi Experience

My Ekadashi Experience

Hare krishna Prabhujis..
Today i want to share some of my experiences of my first ekadashi fasting being at home on 30th July.

As mentioned in scriptures.. ideally one must observe ekadashi, without water and food(nirjala ekadashi), but according to one's capacity, they can do so in stages, like
1.Nirjala fasting
2.Just having some milk and fruits
3.Having ekadashi prasadam i.e. without pulses, seeds, onion garlic, meat etc ..
I, according to my ability, observed the 3rd oneðŸ˜…. All of this is done for two reasons. First for Bodily well being, it helps control the fat buildup, bacterial growth, regulation of our digestion system, and many more.. It also develops our will power to a certain extent, to avoid being tempted by our Hunger stricken thoughtsðŸ˜‹

But, more importantly, it functions on a spiritual platform..It is the 11th day of cycle of both waxing and waning moons, and it is described that if a person observes ekadashi, he is freed from his sins, and he makes swift progress on the path of his spiritual life.It is a must do if one needs to take his spirituality seriously. The start of the day was good, I woke up early, went for a walk, took some fruits and milk, and continued with my regular routine..

But as the day passed, I slowly began to be tempted by all the snacks and the tasty meal my mother had prepared. Then too, I somehow controlled, and made it through the whole day. Next morning, when it was time for me to break the fast, I bathed and was very very eager to eat something delicious, except sabudana and all the upvas items..
Now, a thought crossed my mind, and I wondered upon all of our spiritual fast that we are observing ever since we are into this material world....It was so severe, fasting from all that we actually enjoy as spirit souls, but this fast takes us away from the Lord and close to Maya. From time immemorial, we have been fasting from knowledge of our spiritual existence, from hearing the glories of Supreme Lord and his devotees, from serving them, from studying the scriptures and putting our mind and senses into proper work, and most importantly, fasting from the most merciful, most accessible form of Krishna in the form of chanting His Holy names.

We have been away from it for so long, that we have gradually almost forgotten about what all tasty preparations we have for us, but we are enjoying the mundane savories of materialism. But now, when we are so fortunate, that we have begot such wonderful opportunity, having the chance to associate with devotees, having the fortune to learn more about our Spirituality & The Supreme Personality Of Godhead & his pastimes, we must be very eager to break our dreadful fast of ignorance and disbelief, and savour all that is transcendentally tasty and necessary as our real nature of being a soul..

We must be practically feasting on all of this. The nectarin process of chanting is like the main course, that satisfies our hunger, and we all, at this point, must realize its importance and its potency, and start to practice it in a better way than we already are, trying to be immersed in remembering The Lord.

Hare Krishna
All glories to Srila Prabhupad.

- Viraj Takale
